Galvanized Strapping Buckles


KARAM Galvanized buckles are used with Composite Polyester Strapping to receive the best results. Buckles come in the same size as strapping. If you have 13mm WIDE strapping - Use a 13mm buckle. These Galvanized buckles are manufactured to match the widths of Composite Polyester Strapping that we supply as well.
 Material - Galvanized Steel

Use with Composite Polyester Strapping

Sizes - 13mm, 16mm, 19mm, 25mm and 32mm

Quantity - 250, 500 or 1000 per box (Varies on Buckle size)


Strapping Seals (Grippers)

KARAM Seals are used with Polypropylene or PET - Polyester strapping for securing heavy loads. Seals (Grippers) get their name from the teeth within the seal that is designed to prevent slippage.  

Suitable for Polypropylene (PP) or  PET - Polyester Strapping (PET)

Teeth for an increased grip

Sizes - 13mm or 19mm
 Breaking strength can range up to 660kg with SureFast Extruded Polyester Strapping

Quantity 2000, 2500 per pack(Varies on Seals (Gripper) size)
