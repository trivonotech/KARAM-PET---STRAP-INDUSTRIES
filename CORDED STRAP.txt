CORDED POLYESTER STRAPPING ( CORD STRAP ) 

Corded Polyester Strapping, also known as cord strap, is made out of glued together polyester filaments which provide a high strength strapping system offering up to 1250kg of linear breaking . 
Corded Polyester is also one of the newest, most innovative strapping materials and is most often used for securing heavy pallets, containers or bulky industrial loads. Corded Polyester strapping is a strong and safe material used in a variety of industrial applications

Available Widths - 13mm, 16mm, 19mm, 25mm, 32mm
Available Lengths - 100m up to 1100m
Breaking Strain is Between 375kg and 2000kg
